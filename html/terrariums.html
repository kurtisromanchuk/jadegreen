Terrariums

Jade Green brings natural decor and living plants arrangements to your special event, workspace or home. Custom, hand-made terrariums are made in sealed, glass vessels that only need the occasional watering and are perfectly happy in ambient lighting. Indoor plant arrangements are designed with sustainability and green initiatives in mind. Floral decor and miniature ecosystems are a great way to bring nature into your home, working space and special event.

We push green values and initiatives with our terrariums.

Jade Green is based on the zero waste movement and promotes actions towards a sustainable future by adopting, sharing and connecting green initiatives. Green initiatives include sourcing locally, recycling in as many different ways as possible and refraining from the use of plastic wherever possible…all terrariums are created in glass vessels! Jade Green Life aims to connect with local businesses to create custom plant arrangements that are made in Canada.

Collections

Viride

The Viride is the smallest in the Jade Green closed terrarium collection. This closed ecosystem contains river stones, preserved reindeer moss and natural bark wood chips. What is the best feature about this particular closed terrarium? It never has to be opened or watered, only placed in indirect sunlight and enjoy!

Orbus

Orbus is designed to sit perfectly on a shelf or workspace and contains living tropical plant arrangements. This closed ecosystem contains everything it needs to survive in a contained environment and only requires the occasional care. If you would like to know the other natural materials that can be found in your terrarium, please visit the FAQ’s page.

Vitae

The largest in the Jade Green closed terrarium collection is Vitae, it acts as an attracting natural centre piece for your home, special event or workspace. Vitae contains living tropical plants, mosses, river stones and other natural materials that an ecosystem requires to sustain itself. Simply place Vitae in ambient lighting and watch this miniature ecosystem grow!
